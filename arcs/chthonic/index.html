The Chthonic Arc

A luxury cruise with a wedding party sails into an older ceremony. Nine episodes.

Maiden Voyage

First descent. First sensations. The Chthonic welcomes its guests.

Cringetide

Debt, leverage, and the archaeology of something that refuses explanation.

Veil

Deep sites. Whale songs. The threshold between discovery and captivity.

Asunder

The ceremony. The binding. The moment everything changes.

Shower

Pink fog. Fragmented minds. The comfort of forgetting.

Garter

Knowledge as trap. Liberation as entrapment. Selene reveals.

Tessellated

The cracks in control. The depths call. Non-human recognition.

Undergloom

Naditu. Integration. The temptation of willing surrender.

Spiralstorm

The final ritual. The ship breaks. What emerges descends.
