Bliss Blank

Co-Director, Performer, Creative Force

Bliss Blank is the voice and creative force behind the NNPP universe. As co-director, performer, and editorial architect, she conceived Deep Dream State and sustains it through relentless artistry. Her vision shapes every arc; her performances define the project's emotional core. Each role bears her signature blend of precision, authority, and vulnerability.

Her range is extraordinary. In Interrogation, she delivered a shattering performance that left listeners rattled and breathless. In Belle, she turned inward - an intimate, moving portrait that was delicate, disarming, and ultimately devastating. As Stephanie Ravenscroft, she created a character of stunning nuance and psychological depth. Her roles demonstrate not just technical mastery but artistic courage in every line.

Beyond individual performances, Bliss Blank is the project's archivist and curator. She has appeared in hundreds of NNPP productions, bringing the same care to organizing and preserving the vast recording library. She keeps the archive living and accessible, ensuring the project's history remains vital and discoverable.

Every Saturday night at 9 PM EST, she opens the Deep Drop Party - a listening event that has become a ritual gathering space. There, her warmth fuses with her extraordinary skill, transforming recordings into shared experience. Bliss Blank's voice and vision form the connective tissue of the entire Neuralverse. Every world echoes with her cadence; every dream begins when she speaks.

Roles in Deep Dream State

Olivia Naylor in The Chthonic, April in Incognitoh, and Dr. June Lowell in The Sitri Center.
